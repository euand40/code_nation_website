Python Text-Based Game

My First Game:

During my time at Code Nation I created a text-based adventure game with two other students on the course. Using all the techniques I had learned from "Python Fundamentals" and "Project Managment/Planning". Since I enjoy gaming I enjoyed making this, as games design has always been something that has interested me. We had one day to plan the game and two days to code the game. Me and my team members all agreed that the project went well considering the short time frame we had.

Coding the game consisted mainly of if else statements, which were dependent on what the player chose to do. The main storyline of the game consisted of a group of young adults, deciding to stay the night at a haunted house. The young adults decide to use a Ouija board which summons a friendly ghost that speaks of another aggressive ghost in the home called "The Lady in Grey". The player is one of the young adults and must banish the Lady by collecting items to use in a ritual.

We decided to go with the name "Ghoul Busters" as a play on words from Ghost Busters and chose the horror genre as we were making it over the Halloween period. Shard studios has no significance but was chosen to be the name of our group. I would love to do something like this again but try it individually as I could put more time into it and I would be eager to see what I come up with.
